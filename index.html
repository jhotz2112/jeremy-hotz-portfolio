At-Home COPD Monitoring PWA

KoKo PFT LLC, Software Engineer / Product Lead, 02/2022 – 06/2022

Problem

Patients with COPD typically needed a clinic visit to have their lung function measured. KoKo wanted to move that monitoring into the home, which meant building both a patient experience and a physician experience around a medical device, inside a regulated environment where mistakes carry real consequences.

My Role

I served as product lead, owning discovery, scoping, and backlog management in Jira. I wrote detailed product requirements and acceptance criteria, then coordinated sprint execution across a development team and a firmware engineer.

Approach

I architected the integration between the Google Chrome WebUSB API and a physical spirometer device, working with the firmware engineer to update the device so it could talk to a browser in real time. I also did hands-on testing and validation in staging, reviewing every acceptance test before release.

Outcome

We shipped a functioning medical device product with reliable real-time patient data collection, giving physicians remote visibility into patient lung function without requiring an in-person visit.
